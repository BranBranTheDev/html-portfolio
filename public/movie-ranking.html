The Best Movies According to Brandon Rivera

My Top 3 Movies

Avengers: Endgame

This is one of my top favorite movies, as it brings all of my favorite heros together in one action packed film

Talladega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby

This is one of the funniest movies I have ever seen by far, I could rewatch this and never get tired of it

Coco

What makes Coco in my top 3 is the story that is foretold in the movie, as it is inspired by Mexican culture.
This is a well made movie that brings the art of mariachi music and family together
